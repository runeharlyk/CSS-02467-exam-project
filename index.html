Exploring Amazon Movie & TV Reviews

Uncovering patterns and insights in entertainment preferences

About the Dataset

This analysis explores the rich landscape of Amazon's Movie and TV show reviews, offering insights into viewer preferences, rating patterns, and review networks.

Key Dataset Properties
  • Time period covered: May. 1996 to Sep. 2023
  • Number of reviews: 17.3M
  • Number of unique products: 48.19M
  • Number of unique reviewers: 54.51M
