About the Dataset

Amazon Movies & TV Reviews Dataset

We analyze a curated subset of Amazon's Movies and TV reviews, focusing on verified purchases with helpful votes to ensure data quality.

Processing Steps

  • Normalize metadata (titles, prices, descriptions)
  • Group reviews by movie title
  • Filter for verified purchases with helpful votes
  • Remove reviews with missing titles

Dataset Statistics

  • Final dataset: ~1.6 million reviews
  • Original dataset: 17.3 million reviews
  • Time period: 1996-2023

Network Analysis

Co-Reviewer Network

We connect movies if they share at least 3 common reviewers, creating a network that reveals natural clusters of movies with similar audiences.

Network Visualization

Community Ratings Distribution

Network Structure

  • 10,897 nodes (movies)
  • 25,988 edges
  • Edge weight threshold: >= 3

Community Detection

  • Louvain algorithm (python-louvain)
  • Modularity: 0.767
  • Nodes annotated with title, rating, and price

Text Analysis

Community Text Analysis

We analyze the language patterns within each detected community to understand how different movie clusters are discussed by reviewers.

Text Processing

  • Normalize and tokenize reviews
  • Generate and merge significant bigrams
  • Aggregate tokens by community

Analysis

  • Compute IDF across communities
  • Calculate TF-IDF for five largest communities
  • Generate word clouds for distinctive phrases

Key Insights

Community Structure

  • Clear genre-based clustering with high modularity (0.767)
  • Large communities and vague communities in middle with popular titles with distinct niche communities on the outside.

Audience Behavior

  • Reviewers tend to watch and review similar genres
  • Key words from reviews expands on traditional genres by incorporating user watching patterns
